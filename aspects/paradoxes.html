You Only Live Once and Reincarnation

However, themes constantly repeat.

The sense of truth in both statements helps shed light on Karma. Here's how:

  • This lifetime is perfectly unique and therefore has a moral urgency.
  • This one will lead to the next equally unique and morally urgent lifetime.
  • The quality of uniqueness opens the mind to the Joy and Beauty of the present.
  • The same quality opens ones awareness to Love, to be wholly present and let the past die.
